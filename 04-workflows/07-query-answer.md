# Workflow 7: Query and answer

**Group.** Downstream
**Goal.** Ask the vault a question and get a cited synthesis.

## Steps

1. Human asks a question in Obsidian or terminal.
2. Hermes (writer / researcher) searches literature, claim notes, and reference notes.
3. Synthesizes an answer with citekeys and links.
4. Proposes filing as a synthesis note in `10-inbox/`.
5. Human verifies, edits, and either promotes or discards.

## Owners

Hermes for retrieval and draft. Human for verification and filing.
